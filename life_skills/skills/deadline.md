# Skill: deadline

Allowed kinds: `prep`, `complete`.
- **prep** — start the work / gather what's needed ahead of time.
- **complete** — do or submit the thing itself.

Guidance: For real obligations (renewals, filings, payments) up to 2 — `prep`
then `complete`. For simple ones, just `complete`.
Titles: "Renew the registration", "File taxes", "Prep documents for the renewal".
