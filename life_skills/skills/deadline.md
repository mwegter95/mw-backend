# Skill: deadline

Allowed kinds: `prep`, `complete`.
- **prep** — start the work, gather documents, collect info, budget, or review
  requirements ahead of time.
- **complete** — do, pay, renew, file, submit, return, cancel, register, or finish
  the obligation.

Guidance:
- Real obligations can use up to 2 tasks: `prep` then `complete`.
- Simple bills/renewals/returns often need only `complete`.
- Taxes, applications, registrations, license/passport renewals, insurance,
  school forms, and filings often deserve `prep` + `complete`.
- "RSVP due" should be deadline if the due date is the main point.
- Ignore vague project milestones unless the title clearly states a deliverable.

Titles: "Renew the registration", "File taxes", "Prep documents for the renewal", "Submit the application".
