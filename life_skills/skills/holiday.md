# Skill: holiday

Allowed kinds: `plan`, `shop`, `gift`.
- **plan** — plan the gathering / travel / meal.
- **shop** — groceries / supplies.
- **gift** — presents (Christmas, Valentine's, Mother's/Father's Day).

Guidance: Pick the 1–2 most relevant to the holiday — e.g. Christmas → `gift` +
`plan`; Thanksgiving → `plan` + `shop`; Valentine's Day → `gift`.
Titles: "Plan Thanksgiving", "Shop for Christmas gifts", "Get a Valentine's gift".
