# Skill: social

Allowed kinds: `rsvp`, `plan`, `bring`.
- **rsvp** — respond if it's an invite.
- **plan** — coordinate logistics (where / when / who).
- **bring** — what to bring (dish, drinks, gift).

Guidance: Be conservative — only for NAMED gatherings that genuinely benefit
from prep. Usually one task.
Titles: "RSVP to the housewarming", "Bring a dish to the BBQ".
