# Skill: social

Allowed kinds: `rsvp`, `plan`, `bring`.
- **rsvp** — respond to an invite or confirm attendance.
- **plan** — coordinate logistics: where, when, ride, reservation, sitter, or who.
- **bring** — bring a dish, drinks, gift, card, gear, or supplies.

Guidance:
- Be conservative but do not miss named personal gatherings.
- Use for named/occasion-based gatherings: dinner with a person, party,
  housewarming, shower, BBQ, potluck, game night, date night, family gathering,
  graduation party, hosting, or concert with a named person.
- Usually one task. Use two only when both action types are obvious, like RSVP +
  bring for a shower or plan + bring for hosting.
- Plain "Lunch", "Coffee", "Drinks", or "Hangout" with no named person or purpose
  is ignore.

Titles: "RSVP to the housewarming", "Bring a dish to the BBQ", "Plan dinner with Ashley".
