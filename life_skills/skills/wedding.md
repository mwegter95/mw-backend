# Skill: wedding

Allowed kinds: `rsvp`, `gift`, `outfit`, `travel`.
- **rsvp** — respond to the invite (earliest).
- **gift** — buy from the registry / give a gift.
- **outfit** — sort out what to wear.
- **travel** — arrange travel/lodging if it's out of town.

Guidance: Pick the 1–2 most useful — often `gift` + `rsvp`, or `travel` +
`outfit` when it's away.
Titles: "RSVP to Jenna's wedding", "Buy a wedding gift", "Plan travel for the wedding".
