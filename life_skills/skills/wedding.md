# Skill: wedding

Allowed kinds: `rsvp`, `gift`, `outfit`, `travel`.
- **rsvp** — respond to the invitation or confirm attendance.
- **gift** — buy from the registry, send a gift, or prepare a card.
- **outfit** — sort out clothing, shoes, tailoring, or dress code.
- **travel** — arrange lodging, transportation, time off, or plans if out of town.

Guidance:
- Pick the 1–2 most useful. Common pairs: `rsvp` + `gift`, `travel` + `outfit`,
  or `gift` + `outfit` for local weddings.
- If the event is out of town, multi-day, includes a hotel/flight, or has a
  destination location, use `travel`.
- Rehearsal dinner, ceremony, reception, bridal shower, and wedding weekend are
  wedding-related when a couple/occasion is clear.
- Do not create all four tasks; choose the likely highest-value reminders.

Titles: "RSVP to Jenna's wedding", "Buy a wedding gift", "Plan wedding travel", "Choose an outfit for the wedding".
