# Skill: anniversary

Allowed kinds: `gift`, `plan`, `message`.
- **gift** — a present or gesture.
- **plan** — book/arrange something (dinner, getaway).
- **message** — a simple day-of note (for someone else's anniversary).

Guidance: For the user's own/partner anniversary, prefer `plan` (optionally +
`gift`). Up to 2.
Titles: "Plan our anniversary dinner", "Get an anniversary gift".
