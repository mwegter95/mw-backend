# Skill: birthday

Allowed kinds: `gift`, `plan`, `message`.
- **gift** тАФ buy/prepare a present. Use for close people (family, partner, close friends).
- **plan** тАФ arrange a celebration, dinner, or call.
- **message** тАФ a day-of "wish them well" nudge. Use alone for acquaintances/coworkers.

Guidance: For close people emit up to 2 (usually `gift` + `plan`). For
acquaintances, a single `message`. Pull the person's name from the title.
Titles: "Buy Dad a birthday gift", "Plan Sarah's birthday dinner", "Wish Alex a happy birthday".
