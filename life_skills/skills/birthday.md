# Skill: birthday

Allowed kinds: `gift`, `plan`, `message`.
- **gift** тАФ buy/prepare a present. Use for close people: family, partner, close
  friends, or any relationship implied by "Mom", "Dad", "wife", "husband",
  "grandma", "brother", etc.
- **plan** тАФ arrange a celebration, dinner, call, card, or small gesture.
- **message** тАФ a simple wish-them-well nudge. Use alone for acquaintances,
  coworkers, distant contacts, or low-context birthdays.

Guidance:
- Close person: usually `gift` + `plan`.
- Partner/spouse/parent/child: prefer `plan` and consider `gift`.
- Acquaintance/coworker/unknown calendar birthday: usually just `message`, or
  omit if the person is not meaningful enough for a dashboard reminder.
- Birthday party events may use `plan`/`gift` if attending requires prep.
- Pull the person's name or relationship from the title.

Titles: "Buy Dad a birthday gift", "Plan Sarah's birthday dinner", "Wish Alex a happy birthday".
